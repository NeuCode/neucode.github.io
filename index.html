Our Mission

We aren’t another club that expects top results from math and science talent. Our focus is creating new things, whether it’s benefiting oneself or the community at large.

Project-based Learning

Have an idea? We can help you with that! Here at NeuCode, we teach you fundamental coding skills, get you to meet other coders, and work on that next life changing app idea.

Giving Back

We believe in the power of knowledge. NeuCode helps you use your knowledge to give back and help your community in any way you see fit.
